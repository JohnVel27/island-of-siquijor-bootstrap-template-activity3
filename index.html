About

Welcome to Siquijor

Siquijor, known as the "Island of Fire," is a magical place located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. Its serene beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush mountains make it a perfect destination for those seeking tranquility and adventure alike. The island is rich in history, culture, and natural beauty, attracting visitors from all over the world. Whether you're exploring mystical waterfalls, relaxing on pristine shores, or experiencing the vibrant local traditions, Siquijor offers an unforgettable experience.

Siquijor's charm lies in its unspoiled landscapes and welcoming locals. The island is famous for its ancient healing practices, with herbal medicine and spiritual rituals passed down through generations. The relaxed atmosphere, combined with stunning natural wonders such as the famous Cambugahay Falls and the pristine Salagdoong Beach, makes Siquijor a hidden gem for nature lovers and those looking for a unique cultural experience.
